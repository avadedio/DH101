# Accessibility

This site was built by [Ava Dedio] for DH 101.

I have tried to make it accessible by:

All images include descriptive alt text (description of visuals and their signifiance))
Page headings follow a logical numerical hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3) and are not used just for visual styling
Embedded links describe their destination clearly — no "click here" or other ambiguous links
Color contrast has been checked and meets expectations.
